Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions we receive

How can I volunteer with your organization?

We have various volunteering opportunities available. You can apply through our volunteer form on the Get Involved page, or email our volunteer coordinator at volunteers@hopeforall.org.

How are donations used?

85% of all donations go directly to our programs and services, 10% supports administrative costs, and 5% goes toward fundraising efforts. We are committed to transparency in our financial practices.

Can I specify which program my donation supports?

Yes, when making a donation, you can specify which program you'd like to support. If you don't specify, your donation will be used where it's needed most.

Do you offer internships?

Yes, we offer internships in various departments. Please check our Careers page or email info@hopeforall.org for current opportunities.

How can my company partner with you?

We welcome corporate partnerships! Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@hopeforall.org or call extension 103 to discuss opportunities.

Do you operate internationally?

Yes, we have programs in over 20 countries worldwide. Our international headquarters is located in Hope City, with regional offices in Africa, Asia, and South America.
