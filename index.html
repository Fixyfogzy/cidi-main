About Children Impact and Development Initiative

Children Impact and Development Initiative (CIDI) is a non-governmental, non-profit organization that empowers children through welfare and development support, advocating for their rights and opportunities to thrive.

Our Programs

International Day of Education

This we do by visiting communities to emphasize the importance of education, teach literacy skills, distribute writing materials.

Learn More

Quarterly Medical Outreach

Considering the high cost and medical health care in our country, we embark on Free Medical Outreach every quarter where we go along with Medical Personnels and provide Free Health Counselling, Free Blood Pressure and Blood Sugar as well as Malaria Check ups, Free Medicines.

Learn More

International Women's Day

Annually, we carry out needs assessment and use the opportunity to address pressing issues of Women's Health, Family Planning, Self Love & Care as well as Parenting.
