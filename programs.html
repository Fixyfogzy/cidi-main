Education

International Day of Education

Providing quality education to children in remote areas through school construction, teacher training, and educational resources.

Goals & Impact:

  • Build 10 new schools in underserved communities
  • Train 200 local teachers annually
  • Provide scholarships to 500 students each year
  • Increase literacy rates by 40% in target areas

Healthcare

Quarterly Medical Outreach

Establishing medical clinics and providing essential healthcare services to underserved communities with limited access to medical care.

Goals & Impact:

  • Operate 5 mobile medical clinics in remote areas
  • Provide free medical checkups to 10,000 people annually
  • Vaccinate 5,000 children against preventable diseases
  • Train community health workers in 20 villages
